DYEING AUXILIARIES

At Amar Tex Chem, we specialize in manufacturing and supplying high-quality Dyeing auxiliaries for Textile Dyeing process.

Dyeing auxiliaries are crucial for achieving high-quality dyeing results. They enhance dye exhaustion on textile substrates by forming complexes with dyes. In aqueous-based dyeing processes, auxiliaries like chelating agents, dispersing agents, leveling agents, electrolytes, pH controllers, and surfactants interact with dyes on both natural and synthetic fibers.
