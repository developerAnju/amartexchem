EXPORT / ZDHC CERTIFIED

As name indicates Zero Discharge of Hazardous Chemicals.

ZDHC is an organization established in Amsterdam in 2015. It is dedicated to eliminating hazardous chemicals and promoting sustainable alternatives in the textile, and synthetic industries, contributing to a safer and more sustainable future.

ZDHC / EXPORT CERTIFICATION is a powerful platform for change, connecting the global textile industry to enable sustainable way of chemical management.

Binder

In spinning, both antistatic agents and binders play crucial roles. Antistatic agents prevent static buildup during high-speed operations, ensuring smoother processing.
Binders enhance fiber cohesion, aid in moisture retention, and reduce friction, resulting in stronger, more uniform yarns. Together, they improve efficiency, quality, and sustainability in production.
