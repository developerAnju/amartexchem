ANTISTATIC AGENTS

In textile spinning, static electricity can cause various issues such as yarn breakage, attraction of dust and particles. To combat these problems, our antistatic agents for textile spinning are engineered to dissipate static charges, ensuring a safer and more efficient working environment.

At Amar Tex Chem, we specialize in manufacturing and supplying high-quality Antistatic Agents for Textile Spinning. We take pride in ensuring that all our products are widely acclaimed for their balanced composition and exceptional performance. They provide excellent fibre-to-metal lubrication, enhance fibre-to-fibre cohesion for both natural and synthetic fibres, and their blends. Our clients consistently appreciate the outstanding results delivered by our products.
